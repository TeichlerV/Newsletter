New upgrades for your experience!

Top 3 areas of improvement from Wargaming

AI

We're rolling out the more refined version of our AFK system. If a player is inactive for 30 seconds and there is no reaction to the warning received, they will be automatically replaced with an AI partner to enable the squad to keep playing.


Map

Improvements in the map include ripples and splashes when traversing water. A motel area , improved happy little trees, light settings, and a brand new Bus Station! Besides that can you now toggle Motion Blur and Chromatic Aberration effects for the High Graphics preset in the Settings menu.


Audio

One-two, one-two - sound check! Major quality changes in our overall ambience and battle soundtracks, as well as some new sound effects. Flying projectiles now sound more realistic, and you can hear the impact that the heavy metallic body of a fallen Character makes when it hits the ground.
